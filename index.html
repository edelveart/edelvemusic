Edgar Delgado Vega has experience in virtual teaching (music theory and technology, live coding). He has written the article indexed in Scopus: Formal Structures of a Harmony in the Parabola (Mathematics and Computation in Music 2022, Atlanta-USA).

DOI: 10.1007/978-3-031-07015-0_30

He is passionate about technology, since it allows him to apply unifying ideas between: Programming, mathematics and music. He has participated in international festivals and obtained first prizes in the most important guitar competitions in Peru. He is a graduate of the Escuela Nacional Superior de Ballet. In addition, he participated in the First International Ballet School Competition, where he obtained a bronze medal. In his facet as a writer, his collection of poems “Espacio del Loto” earned him the First Special Mention in the David Mejía Velilla International Contest, organized by the Universidad de la Sabana-Colombia.

If you are interested in music technology projects, feel free to contact me by message.
